# Project KPIs

## KPI 1 - Total Customers
Count of all customers.

## KPI 2 - Churn Customers
Count of customers where Churn = Yes.

## KPI 3 - Churn Rate
(Total Churn Customers / Total Customers) × 100

## KPI 4 - Active Customers
Count of customers where Churn = No.

## KPI 5 - Average Monthly Revenue
Average MonthlyCharges.

## KPI 6 - Average Customer Tenure
Average tenure of customers.
