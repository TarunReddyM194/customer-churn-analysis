# Business Problem Statement

## Objective
Analyze customer churn behavior in a telecom company and identify factors that contribute to customer attrition.

## Business Questions

1. What is the overall churn rate?

2. Which customer segments are most likely to churn?

3. How do contract types influence churn?

4. Does monthly billing impact churn?

5. Which services are associated with higher churn?

6. What recommendations can help improve customer retention?

## Expected Outcome

Provide data-driven insights and recommendations to reduce customer churn and improve customer retention.
